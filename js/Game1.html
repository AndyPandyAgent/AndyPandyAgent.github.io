Narravtive Design

The narrative design process consisted of a creative decision making process between me and the artist.
The artist had all these ideas behind the characters they were making and I wanted to show these characters the best way possible in the story.
It was a lot about asking them how they thought they would react, what feelings they would feel and how they spoke.
The rest was just me writing and coming up with situations and choices for the player.


Choice tree

The choice tree was made to clearly visualize and clarify as a designer how everything looked
The tree was changed a lot throughout the project due to schedule.
We were very ambitious but quickly scaled it down to made deadline


Project Owner

This role was all about management and to make our team one.
Most of the people in this team had never worked with each other before so I tried to make everyone feel welcome and at home every morning and to make sure we got to know each other.
Management wise I organized and keep track of our Trello board and made sure we got things done on time.
