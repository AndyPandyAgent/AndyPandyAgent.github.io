BadDool

Concept

Engane with entertaining NPCs, collect unique cards,
and use them in first person card battles

BadDool is a 2.5D rougle-like card battle
dungeon crawler with fun and unique NPCs.

Project Details

Plattform: PC, Steam
Genre: Rouge-like
Engine: Unity
Development time: 12 weeks

Responsibilities

Game Design

In BadDool I encountered an interesting design problem because of the sheer amount of random generation.
Luckily, with an extreme amount of research I managed to design around this problem.

Gameplay Design

The Gameplay Design consisted of balancing, ideation of features and prototyping
as well as a lot of enemy design.





Level Design

BadDool uses randomly generated dungeons which meant the level design consisted
of using universal ways of guidance. For example, lights on every open door.

Project Management

My main role in this project was to manage the team and make sure the vision and goal are kept throughout the project

Task Management / Scrum

For this project I taught myself Jira and it worked very well.
All our tasks were tracked on Jira and we filled it with tasks every meeting.
I mainly used sprints for every other week and epics for more broad tasks.
