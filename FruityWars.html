Concept

Fruity Wars is a third person action RTS game
where you jump around platforms and
use troops in strategic combat.

Project Details

Plattform: PC
Genre: RTS/Platformer
Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Development time: 7 weeks
Restrictions:
Theme:
"Dangerous to be alone"




Roles and
Responsibilities


Gameplay/Technical Design
Rapid Prototyping, script optimizing and ideating features that
will improve the player experience.

Technical Design

My role as a technical designer consisted of prototyping and blueprint implementation.

Prototyping

Proof of concept

On the first day I prototyped a very simple version of the game
in its purest form to prove the concept.

Control testing

I scripted 6 different controls to test which
one felt best before we told the programmers.

Mechanics

Moving Platform

My level designer wanted a moving platform
so I made one for him. It is capabale of infinte points
and it has both ping pong mode and full circle.
It also slowes down and stops when it reaches the end and start.

Disappearing Platform

We needed a way to get the player into combat.
I prototyped a disappearing platform.
We liked the feature so I added more features to it
and implemeted it into the game

Technical Gameplay Design

I was given controll over the 3Cs and the programming behind it

Jump



I scripted and maintained a coyote jump to make jump timming more forgiving. A buffer jump to make quick jump easier and sticky feet to make landing smoother.

Camera



I scripted a slow-motion feature, tweeked the camera zoom-out and worked on camera speed/fluidity.

Dodge



I scripted, balanced and maintained a dash mechanic.
